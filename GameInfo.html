Game info

September 21, 2019

Options:

The game has a total of 10 levels. The user has the option to go in between levels and play in whichever difficulty they desire. Which is what the "Level" tab is for. There is then a "Game" tab in which the user can play straight meaning that in order to get to the next level they have to pass the previous level.

Point of the game

September 29, 2019

The point of the game:

Is to test whether a person is better at recognizing certain speech cues in a sentence with a high amount of background noise. Encourage users to pay attention to pronunciation of certain words in a sentence or phrase. The game gathers data and helps identify what sentence or phrase was easiest said. It helps round up what words are easier to hear.

How to play

October 6, 2019

How to play:

The game automatically starts it will show the points you can get above the game screen for each level as well as your current score. If answered incorrectly it will deduct 100 points, and restart the level. If answered correctly 200 points will be added. The user must listen and try to decipher the sentence or phrase being said. To answer the user types in the answer into the box below the play screen and clicks submit.
